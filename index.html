About June 18th

Jordan was born at 2:46pm on June 17, 1986, in Santa Monica, CA. ~46 hours later and ~100 miles up the coast, Kevin was born at 12:35pm on June 19, 1986, in Santa Barbara, CA. Each year we celebrate the 17th as “Jordan’s birthday,” the 19th as “Kevin’s birthday,” and the 18th as “our birthday.” Now, we are excited to celebrate the 18th as “our wedding” and then as “our anniversary” (the latter being something we will never accidentally forget!).
